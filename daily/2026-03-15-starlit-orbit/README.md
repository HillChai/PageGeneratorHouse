# Starlit Orbit

A quiet night garden where soft orbs drift with the breeze. Click to plant glows, steer the orbit with your cursor, and release lanterns into the sky.

## Interactions
- **Move cursor**: nudge the orbit drift
- **Click/tap**: plant a glow seed
- **Night Glow slider**: adjust aura intensity
- **Breeze slider**: control drift strength
- **Release Lantern**: send a lantern upward
